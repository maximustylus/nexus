# IDC App (Interactive Dashboard for Clinicians) v1.3 [PILOT]

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Pilot%20Phase-orange) ![Org](https://img.shields.io/badge/Unit-Sport%20%26%20Exercise%20Medicine-blue)

The **IDC App** is a **clinician-led innovation project** designed to explore digital solutions for workload management and staff wellbeing at the Sport & Exercise Medicine Centre.

> **Note:** This is a Proof of Concept (POC) application. It is currently being evaluated by the Senior Clinical team for internal efficiency improvements.

## üöÄ Pilot Features

### 1. Workload Visualization
* **Objective:** Move away from static Excel sheets to a dynamic, real-time view of *Clinical, Research, and Education* domains.
* **Goal:** Identify imbalance in staff portfolios early.

### 2. AURA (wellbeing-bot)
* **Objective:** Test the viability of AI-driven "Pulse Checks" to monitor team morale without invasive surveys.
* **Privacy:** All inputs are anonymized during this pilot.

### 3. Rostering Automation
* **Objective:** Reduce the administrative burden of manual scheduling.
* **Current State:** Generates draft rosters for verification by the admin team.
